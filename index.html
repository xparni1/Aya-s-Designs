About Us

Aya's Designs is the result of an effort of selection and classification of contemporary architecture conducted for over fifteen years. Patient work, done with care, image after image, project after project, to offer you the ideal tool with which to organize your knowledge of contemporary architecture. Instead of a quick, distracted web, we want a slow, attentive one. Instead of hastily perused information, we prefer knowledge calmly absorbed. This is why Aya's Designs is a place to perceive architecture slowly, without distractions. no advertising, banners, pop-ups. Just architecture, no more and no less.
